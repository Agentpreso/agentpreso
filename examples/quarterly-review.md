## Challenges and Risks

- Enterprise sales cycle lengthening in EMEA region
  - Average deal cycle increased from 68 to 94 days
  - Mitigation: Hired regional sales director, launching partner channel
- Infrastructure costs growing faster than revenue
  - Gross margin compressed 2 points to 71%
  - Mitigation: Migration to multi-tenant architecture in Q1
- Key competitor raised $120M Series C
  - Increased marketing spend in our core segments
  - Mitigation: Accelerating product differentiation roadmap
